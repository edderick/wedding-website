Accomodation & Taxis

Accomodation

Since the on-site AirBnB has already been booked up, we reccomend you book alternative accomodation. To the best of our knowledge, this is the only AirBnB in the village. There are a few other AirBnBs near-by, but we're certain they're terrible so don't stay in them (or do and leave a bad review!)

There are a few hotels near Offham, some of which claim to be in Sevenoaks but are actually in Wrotham Heath. These luxury establishments boast incredible proximity to the M26 Junction on the A20! These hotels are probably slightly beyond a safe walking distance after midnight:

  • Holiday Inn (The Posthouse) - 2.1 miles
  • Premier Inn (The Beafeater) - 1.9 miles

In the direction of Maidstone, Larkfield has two hotels:

  • Premier Inn (Castle Lake) - 2.9 miles
  • Priory Hotel - 2.1 miles

Maidstone has many more hotels, including an award winning Travelodge! Finding these is an excercise left for the reader.

There are also a few 'boutique' hotels and B&Bs in the area. Form a quick Google search, there are around eigth hotel rooms in West Malling. We've actually just booked one so now there are only seven.

We are still negotiating camping space with the venue management, watch this space for more developments on this.
